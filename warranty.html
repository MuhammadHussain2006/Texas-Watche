WARRANTY COVERAGE DETAILS

Understanding what's included and excluded in your warranty coverage

WHAT'S COVERED

Manufacturing Defects

Faulty components, assembly errors, movement malfunctions

Movement Issues

Timing problems, power reserve issues, automatic winding failures

Crown & Pushers

Defective crowns, chronograph pushers, date quickset mechanisms

Crystal & Dial

Sapphire crystal defects, dial printing errors, hand alignment issues

Bracelet & Clasp

Faulty clasps, bracelet link failures, deployment buckle issues

WHAT'S NOT COVERED

Accidental Damage

Impact damage, drops, crushing, water damage from misuse

Normal Wear & Tear

Scratches, scuffs, aging of luminous material, leather strap wear

Unauthorized Service

Repairs by non-authorized technicians, replacement with non-genuine parts

Misuse & Abuse

Exposure beyond specifications, magnetic damage, chemical exposure

Consumable Items

Batteries, rubber gaskets, leather straps, polish and cleaning
