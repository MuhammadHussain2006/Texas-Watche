OUR VERIFICATION PROCESS

Multi-step authentication ensures every timepiece meets our standards

1

Source Verification

We only source from authorized dealers, established collectors, and verified estate sales. Every watch's provenance is documented and verified.

2

Physical Inspection

Certified horologists examine every component including case, movement, dial, hands, and crown for authenticity markers and manufacturing quality.

3

Serial Number Verification

All serial numbers are cross-referenced with manufacturer databases and verified against known authentic timepieces.

4

Advanced Testing

Specialized equipment tests movement accuracy, water resistance, and magnetic resistance to manufacturer specifications.

5

Final Certification

Upon passing all tests, watches receive our authenticity certificate and are photographed for our permanent verification database.

PROFESSIONAL AUTHENTICATION TOOLS

Advanced equipment and expertise ensure accurate verification

MICROSCOPIC ANALYSIS

High-powered microscopes reveal manufacturing marks, engravings, and surface treatments unique to authentic timepieces.

PRECISION WEIGHING

Precise measurements of weight, dimensions, and component specifications are compared against manufacturer standards.

UV LIGHT TESTING

Ultraviolet light reveals luminous materials and fluorescent markers that confirm authentic manufacturing processes.

MAGNETIC TESTING

Magnetic field testing verifies anti-magnetic properties and movement specifications claimed by manufacturers.

DATABASE VERIFICATION

Serial numbers and model references are verified against comprehensive manufacturer and auction databases.

MOVEMENT ANALYSIS

Timing machines and movement analyzers verify mechanical performance and authentic caliber specifications.

AUTHENTIC VS COUNTERFEIT

Key differences that separate genuine luxury watches from fakes

Feature Authentic Counterfeit
Weight Substantial, precise weight Often lighter, inconsistent
Movement Smooth, precise sweep Ticking, irregular motion
Engravings Crisp, deep, perfect Shallow, blurry, uneven
Materials Premium metals, sapphire Cheap alloys, mineral glass
Crown Smooth operation, proper logo Rough, misaligned, poor logo
Dial Perfect printing, alignment Smudged, crooked, faded
Bracelet Solid links, smooth action Hollow links, rough edges
Serial Numbers Unique, verifiable Duplicate, fake, missing
