PRIVACY NOTICE

At Texas Watches, we recognize the paramount importance of protecting the privacy of your personal information. We have instituted comprehensive policies and robust security measures to safeguard the information you entrust to us.

When you register an account with us, you provide your explicit consent to process your submitted personal data in accordance with this privacy policy. This policy applies to all protected personal information collected, maintained, or used by Texas Watches in connection with our luxury timepiece services.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. Contact us at privacy@texas-sales.com for any privacy-related inquiries.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect personal data from visitors who register their information to create an account with Texas Watches. This information may include:

Personal Information

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth

Address Information

  • Billing address
  • Shipping address
  • Country/Region
  • ZIP/Postal code

We also collect information about your interactions with our website, including browsing patterns, purchase history, and preferences to enhance your shopping experience.

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

With your explicit consent, we use your personal information for the following purposes:

Order Processing

To process and fulfill your luxury watch orders, including payment processing and shipping arrangements.

Communication

To keep you informed about special events, promotions, new arrivals, and important updates about your orders.

Service Improvement

To review our performance, improve our services, and enhance your overall shopping experience.

Customer Support

To provide customer service, respond to inquiries, and resolve any issues related to your purchases.

INFORMATION SHARING

Our Commitment

We will never sell, rent, or disclose our clients' names, addresses, email addresses, or other personal information to any third-party entity.

Your personal information remains confidential and is used exclusively for the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. We maintain strict internal policies to ensure your data is protected.

Limited Exceptions

We may disclose your information only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • In connection with a legal claim or proceeding
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

SECURITY MEASURES

We employ industry-standard security practices to protect your personal information:

Encryption

All sensitive data is encrypted using advanced encryption standards (AES) both in transit and at rest.

Firewalls

Multiple layers of firewall protection prevent unauthorized access to our systems.

Access Control

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access customer data.

Data Retention

Customer data is automatically deleted after one month of successful order delivery.

Additional Protection

We never distribute or rent your personal information to third parties unless required by law. Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

PROTECTION OF MINORS

Texas Watches does not knowingly collect or maintain personal information from individuals under the legal age of 18, and no part of our website is directed toward minors.

Important Notice for Minors

If you are under 18 years of age, please do not use or access our website at any time or in any manner.

If we become aware that personal information from a person under 18 has been collected without proper parental or guardian consent, we will take immediate action to delete this information from our systems.

Parents and guardians who believe their child has provided personal information to us should contact us immediately at privacy@texas-sales.com.

POLICY UPDATES

This Privacy Policy is subject to periodic review and updates to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We reserve the right to modify, change, or update this policy at any time.

Notification of Changes

When significant changes are made to this policy, we will notify you via email and post a notice on our website. Continued use of our services after such notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Any changes, updates, or modifications will be effective immediately upon posting on our website. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.

This Privacy Policy does not create any contractual or other legal rights in or on behalf of any party.

QUESTIONS ABOUT THIS POLICY?

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
