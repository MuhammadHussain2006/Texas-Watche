CARE INSTRUCTIONS

Preserve the legacy of your luxury timepiece

PROTECT YOUR INVESTMENT

Proper care and maintenance ensure your luxury timepiece remains accurate, beautiful, and valuable for generations. Follow these expert guidelines to preserve your watch's performance and appearance.

WATER RESISTANCE

Understand your watch's water resistance rating and maintain gasket integrity through regular servicing and proper crown operation.

IMPACT PROTECTION

Protect your timepiece from shocks, magnetic fields, and extreme temperatures to maintain accuracy and prevent damage.

REGULAR SERVICING

Schedule professional maintenance every 3-5 years to ensure optimal performance and longevity of your luxury timepiece.

DAILY CARE ROUTINE

Simple habits that will keep your timepiece in pristine condition

Morning Routine

Daily

Wind your mechanical watch gently (25-30 turns) and check the time accuracy. Inspect the case and bracelet for any visible damage or wear.

Handling

Daily

Always handle your watch with clean, dry hands. Avoid touching the crystal and operate the crown and pushers gently to prevent damage.

Evening Storage

Daily

Store your watch in a dedicated watch box or soft cloth pouch. Keep it away from magnetic fields, extreme temperatures, and direct sunlight.

CARE BY WATCH TYPE

Specialized care instructions for different watch categories

DRESS WATCH CARE

Delicate Movement: Handle with extra care

Thin cases require gentle handling and protection from impacts

Limited Water Resistance: Avoid water exposure

Most dress watches are not designed for water activities

Precious Materials: Special cleaning required

Gold, platinum, and leather require specific care methods

SPORT WATCH CARE

Active Use: Clean after workouts

Remove sweat and dirt to prevent corrosion and odor

Water Resistance: Maintain gasket integrity

Regular pressure testing ensures continued water resistance

Robust Construction: Can handle more activity

Built for active lifestyles but still requires proper care

DIVE WATCH CARE

Saltwater Exposure: Rinse thoroughly after use

Salt can corrode metal components over time

Pressure Testing: Annual water resistance check

Ensure gaskets maintain proper sealing under pressure

Rotating Bezel: Keep mechanism clean and lubricated

Sand and debris can impair bezel rotation

CHRONOGRAPH CARE

Complex Mechanism: Use chronograph regularly

Periodic use prevents lubricants from settling

Pusher Operation: Press firmly but gently

Avoid half-presses that can damage the mechanism

Service Frequency: More frequent servicing needed

Complex movements require expert attention every 3-4 years

Deep clean bracelet, check crown operation, and test water resistance if applicable.

PROPER CLEANING TECHNIQUES

Safe methods to keep your timepiece sparkling clean

CASE CLEANING

Materials Needed

  • Microfiber cloth
  • Soft-bristled toothbrush
  • Mild soap solution
  • Distilled water

Step-by-Step Process

  1. 1. Remove watch from wrist and ensure crown is sealed
  2. 2. Dampen microfiber cloth with distilled water
  3. 3. Gently wipe case surface in circular motions
  4. 4. Use soft brush for intricate areas
  5. 5. Dry thoroughly with clean microfiber cloth

BRACELET CLEANING

Metal Bracelets

  • Soak in warm soapy water for 5 minutes
  • Use soft brush between links
  • Rinse with distilled water
  • Dry completely with soft cloth

Leather Straps

  • Wipe with slightly damp cloth only
  • Use leather conditioner monthly
  • Avoid water and excessive moisture
  • Replace when worn or damaged

CRYSTAL CLEANING

Sapphire Crystal

Most luxury watches feature sapphire crystal, which is scratch-resistant but requires proper care.

  • Use lint-free microfiber cloth
  • Circular motions from center outward
  • Avoid abrasive cleaners

Never Use

  • Paper towels or tissues
  • Household cleaners
  • Abrasive compounds
  • Ultrasonic cleaners
