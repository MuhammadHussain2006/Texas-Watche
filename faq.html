ORDERING & PAYMENT

How do I place an order?

Placing an order is simple. Browse our collection, select your desired watch, choose any options, and click "Add to Cart". Review your items in the shopping cart, provide shipping information, and complete payment. You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely using SSL encryption to protect your financial information.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 24 hours of placement, provided they haven't been shipped. Contact our customer service team immediately at sales@texas-sales.com or +1 (555) 839-2792 for assistance.

Do you offer installment payments?

Currently, we require full payment at the time of order. However, we're exploring financing options for future implementation. Contact us for information about payment plans for high-value purchases.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free worldwide shipping on all orders with no minimum purchase required. All shipments are fully insured and tracked for your peace of mind.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by destination:

  • Domestic (US): 3-5 business days
  • International: 15-25 business days
  • Processing time: 1-3 business days

How can I track my order?

You'll receive a tracking number via email within 72 hours of shipment. Use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's tracking portal. You'll also receive updates on delivery progress.

What if my package is lost or damaged?

All shipments are fully insured. If your package is lost or damaged during transit, contact us immediately. We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your watch or a full refund.

RETURNS & REFUNDS

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be in original, unworn condition with all original packaging, tags, and documentation. Contact us before returning any item to receive return authorization.

How do I return an item?

To return an item:

  1. Contact customer service for return authorization
  2. Package the item securely with all original materials
  3. Use trackable, insured shipping service
  4. We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days

Who pays for return shipping?

For returns due to personal preference or sizing issues, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. For defective or damaged items, we'll provide a prepaid return label and cover all shipping costs.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, we offer exchanges for different models or sizes, subject to availability. Contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange. Price differences may apply for upgraded models.

PRODUCT INFORMATION

Are all watches authentic?

Absolutely! We guarantee 100% authenticity on all timepieces. Every watch comes with proper documentation, certificates of authenticity, and original packaging. We source directly from authorized dealers and manufacturers.

Do watches come with warranty?

Yes, all watches come with the manufacturer's warranty. Warranty terms vary by brand and model, typically ranging from 2-5 years. We provide warranty coordination and can facilitate service with authorized centers.

Can I see detailed specifications?

Detailed specifications are available on each product page, including case material, movement type, water resistance, dimensions, and features. Contact us if you need additional technical information.

Do you offer customization or engraving?

Customization options vary by brand and model. Some manufacturers offer engraving services or custom straps. Contact us with your specific requirements, and we'll check availability and pricing for custom options.

ACCOUNT & SUPPORT

Do I need to create an account to order?

While you can checkout as a guest, creating an account allows you to track orders, save favorites, access order history, and receive exclusive offers. It only takes a minute to register.

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team through:

  • Email: sales@texas-sales.com (24-hour response)
  • Phone: +1 (555) 839-2792 (Mon-Fri 9AM-6PM)
  • Contact Form: Available on our contact page

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we take privacy and security seriously. All personal and payment information is encrypted using SSL technology. We never share your information with third parties. Review our Privacy Policy for complete details.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk pricing?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for qualified retailers and bulk orders. Contact our business development team at sales@texas-sales.com with your requirements for a custom quote.
